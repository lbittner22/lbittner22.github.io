Why Resident Evil 7 stands out

RE7 shifts the series into first-person and focuses on one creepy location: the Baker family estate. It’s slower, more intimate horror instead of just action.

  • First-person perspective that makes everything feel close and personal.
  • A single, detailed location full of shortcuts and secrets.
  • Gross body horror and tense chases with the Bakers.

What I like about it

RE7 works because it constantly makes you feel trapped. The house layout, tight hallways, and the Bakers showing up when you think you’re safe keep the tension high the whole time.
